Selection Process

Follow the below steps

Step : 1

The third year students can apply for this competition by filling their details in a google form link, this is a group project and a group can have maximum of 5 students and minimum of 3 students so that they can all get an opportunity to showcase their talent and skills.

Step : 2

The students who are registered have to submit a code for their website and a ppt for explaining their point of views.

Step : 3

We will check all the projects submitted by the students in a group and we will enhance the top groups and give internship to those students..
